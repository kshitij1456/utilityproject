The three applications of UtilityPlus offer one powerful solution.
Local governments can support the community while getting repairs done, and receiving taxes and utility payments in a timelier manner.
Utilities
This App utilities/services such as natural gas, water, electricity, and sewer charges. Utilities accepts payments using credit or debit cards, bank drafts, ACH, check and cash payments.
Utilities includes the mobile Meter Reader feature that facilitates the gathering of meter readings while feeding the information directly into the Utilities application.
Utilities can communicate directly with printing companies to print monthly bills, or allow printing in house.
Occupational Licenses

The Occupational License application lets business owners apply for an occupational license online.

Business owners can pay the license fees online and print out their official occupational license.
